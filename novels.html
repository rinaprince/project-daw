A little introduction.

Also known as Fushigi Yûgi Gaiden, there are a total of eleven novels (although finally there are thirteen books, since two of them are doubles) that have stories written by Nishizaki Megumi.

Most of the novels, specifically nine, correspond to stories focused on certain characters, to delve into them more than the manga does.

Novels

Character novels.

Tasuki - Genrou den (January 30th at 1998)

Plot:
Young Shun'u Kou runs away from home fed up with being the errand boy for his tyrannical older sisters. On Mount Leikaku he is assaulted by sleazy thieves who are about to leave him with nothing when a handsome young warrior called Hakurou rescues him and uses a flame-throwing tessen against them. This is the leader of the bandits of that mountain and the young man is so admired that he insists that he accept him in his band. Hakurou refuses and turns his back on him, but the young man manages to arrive before himself to his strength and in the face of such determination, he has no choice but to accept it, for which he places him under the tutelage of his young subordinate, Kouji.

Chiriko - Yunai den (April 26th at 2002)

Plot:
Suzaku's summoning ceremony was initially sabotaged because one of the Stars of Seiryû impersonated Chiriko, Suzaku's last chosen one who was yet to appear. This novel tells both what happened those days in the life of the young genius, as well as the awakening of his power a few years before and the problems that he generated not being able to control it.

OVA's Novels

Two books were published that told another story, a sequel to the finished story of the manga and that had their animated version in OVA format: Eikou den.

Eikou den 1 (February 1rst at 2000)

Eikou den 2 (March 1rst at 2000)

Plot:
The story begins with the expected image of the wedding between Miaka and Taka. In the front row there are six empty seats that are reserved for the other Suzaku stars. Although everything is happiness, there is someone who is not happy about the wedding: Mayo, one of the girls that Taka trains and who is in love with him. She finds out the story of the magic book and takes it from the library and, although Keisuke tries to dissuade her, she dreams of being the priestess of Suzaku and that her guardian is Tamahome. She is more and more convinced that the story is real and after learning that Miaka and Taka are expecting her first child, she ignores Keisuke's pleas and opens the book, ready to change the story.

Sanbou den 1 (July 1rst at 2003)

Sanbou den 2 (September 2nd at 2003)

Plot:
The story is located between the two parts that make up the manga, that is, between volumes 13 and 14. Its protagonists are Tasuki and Chichiri, the only stars left alive, apart from Tamahome who is in the real world.
